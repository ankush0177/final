About


Montreal Bakery is one of the best cake delivery in Montreal. It is committed to making your special day memorable by bringing zeal and happiness together. They aim to offer hassle-free cake services and their delivery to their customers. It has created an almost infinite range of cake styles that can all be personalised to create a unique centerpiece for any event. Just Bake is entirely reliable and making your special day unforgettable by bringing together passion and joy. All their cakes are handmade and use only the best high-quality ingredients to make the tastiest cakes. You can choose from their hundreds of designs and customise them with your text or photos. They do not endorse artificial flavors and food colorings but only use real fruit ingredients to make their cakes. Online cake delivery services are available.

SPECIALITY: Fathers Day, Mocha Magic Large, Birthday, Red Velvet, Dark Brown, Irish Coffee, White Forest Large, Premium Butter Scotch, Guitar, Lego, Chess, Blueberry Gateaux Small, Pen Cake, Rock Band, Cricket Stadium, Kite, Drum, Swimming Pool & Yellow Fancy Cakes
